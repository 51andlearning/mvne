# Assumptions

## Data & Content

- All content is sourced from the MVNO Workshop Presentations v11.pptx and GCT MVNE MVNO Proposal 20260128.docx provided by MVNE.
- No internal system data, live API data, or CRM data is accessed.
- Financial figures and projections are directional and for proposal purposes only.
- Subscriber targets (1M within 3 years, 2M within 5 years) are strategic estimates requiring post-approval validation.

## Technical

- Site is statically rendered — no backend, database, or authentication required at launch.
- Images from source PPTX are not embedded; placeholder or brand-approved images are used.
- Signature blocks are presentational only — no e-signature integration at this stage.

## Commercial

- All pricing, ARPU, and commercial terms require formal sign-off before being treated as binding.
- This site does not constitute a legally binding agreement until both parties execute the Signatures section.

## Post-Approval Validation Required

- Regulatory compliance details (NCC, NIMC, NDPA) must be verified by legal counsel.
- Wholesale rate appendices referenced in the source document are not published on this site.
- Partner logos and brand assets require rights clearance before public deployment.
